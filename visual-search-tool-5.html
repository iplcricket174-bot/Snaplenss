Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SnapLens

What is SnapLens? +

SnapLens is a free Google Lens alternative that runs in your browser. Upload any image to extract text (OCR), detect objects, identify dominant colors, and find visually similar images on Google, Bing, Yandex and TinEye — no login required.

Is SnapLens completely free? +

Yes! SnapLens is 100% free to use. No account, no subscription, no hidden fees. Just upload your image and get instant results.

How does SnapLens OCR work? +

SnapLens uses Tesseract.js — a powerful open-source OCR engine — to extract text from images directly in your browser. It supports printed text, screenshots, scanned documents and more.

Can I use SnapLens on my phone? +

Absolutely! SnapLens is fully mobile-responsive and works on all Android and iPhone browsers. No app download needed — just open snaplens.vercel.app in your browser.

What is reverse image search? +

Reverse image search lets you upload an image instead of typing text to find where it appears online, find similar images, identify products, check if a photo is real, and more. SnapLens connects you to Google, Bing, Yandex and TinEye for reverse search.

About SnapLens — The Best Free Google Lens Alternative

SnapLens is a powerful free visual search tool designed as the best Google Lens alternative available online. Whether you want to search by image, extract text from a photo, detect objects in an image, or find similar images across the web — SnapLens does it all, instantly and for free.

Unlike Google Lens which requires a Google account and the Google app, SnapLens works directly in any browser — on mobile or desktop — with no login required. Simply upload your image or paste an image URL to get started.

SnapLens uses advanced AI technology including Tesseract OCR for text extraction, canvas-based image analysis for object and color detection, and direct integrations with Google Images, Bing Visual Search, Yandex Images and TinEye for reverse image search.
